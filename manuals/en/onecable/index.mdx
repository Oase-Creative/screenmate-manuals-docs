## What is Screenmate OneCable?

The Screenmate OneCable is a portable monitor designed to boost your productivity by adding a second screen to your laptop with just a single cable connection. It's perfect for work, travel, and entertainment.

### Key Features

- **15.6" Full HD Display** - Crystal clear 1920x1080 IPS screen
- **One Cable Solution** - Power, video, and data through a single USB-C cable
- **Universal Compatibility** - Works with Windows, MacOS, and more
- **Ultra-Portable** - Only ~800g, fits in your laptop bag
- **Plug & Play** - No complicated setup required
