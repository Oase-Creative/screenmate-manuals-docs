## Safety Information

Please read these instructions carefully before using the monitor.

-   **Environment**: Do not expose the monitor to rain, moisture, or extreme temperatures.
-   **Cleaning**: Use a soft, dry cloth to clean the screen. Do not use chemical cleaners or alcohol.
-   **Handling**: Be careful not to drop the monitor or apply excessive pressure to the screen.
-   **Power**: Use only the provided cables and recommended power sources.
