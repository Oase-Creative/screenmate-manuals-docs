## Installation & Connection

### 1. Installation Instructions
Place the monitor on a stable surface and unfold the stand.

<img src="/images/Screenmate%20-%20OneCable%20-%20Handleiding%20images/Installatie%20instructies.png" alt="Installation overview" className="rounded-lg border border-gray-200" />

### 2. Connecting via USB-C (Recommended)
The easiest way to connect the Screenmate OneCable is using the included USB-C cable. This provides both power and video signal to the monitor.

**Steps:**
1. Connect the USB-C cable to your laptop's USB-C port.
2. Connect the other end to the 'Type-C' port on the monitor.
3. The monitor will turn on automatically.

<img src="/images/Screenmate%20-%20OneCable%20-%20Handleiding%20images/USB-C%20Poort.png" alt="USB-C Port" className="rounded-lg border border-gray-200" />

<Note>
**Note:** Your laptop must support "DisplayPort Alt Mode" over USB-C. Check your laptop manual if you are unsure.
</Note>

### 4. Charging
If your laptop doesn't provide enough power, or if you want to use the monitor to charge your laptop (Passthrough Charging), connect an external USB-C charger to the PD (Power Delivery) port on the monitor.

<img src="/images/Screenmate%20-%20OneCable%20-%20Handleiding%20images/Opladen%20van%20de.png" alt="Charging" className="rounded-lg border border-gray-200" />

### 5. Use with 2 USB-A Cables (Older Devices)
Does your laptop not have a USB-C port with video support? Use the included USB-A cables.

**Steps:**
1. Connect the **black** USB-A plug for data.
2. Connect the **red** USB-A plug for extra power (if needed).
3. Connect the USB-C end to the monitor.
