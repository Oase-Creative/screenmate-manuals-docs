## Driver installation for Windows

### Step 1: Automatic installation

Connect the Screenmate to your laptop. The drivers will be installed automatically. Make sure your laptop is connected to the internet. Restart your laptop after installation.

<img src="/images/Screenmate%20-%20OneCable%20-%20Handleiding%20images/installation-images/screenmate-onecable-installation-windows-step-1.png" alt="Step 1: Automatic installation" className="rounded-lg border border-gray-200 dark:border-gray-700" />

### Step 2: Manual installation (if needed)

Does the automatic installation not work? Then connect the USB stick. Open the folder that matches your laptop's Windows version.

<img src="/images/Screenmate%20-%20OneCable%20-%20Handleiding%20images/installation-images/screenmate-onecable-installation-windows-step-2.png" alt="Step 2: Connect USB stick" className="rounded-lg border border-gray-200 dark:border-gray-700" />

### Step 3: Install driver

Double-click on RacerDisplayDriver to start the installation. Follow the steps on the screen. Restart your laptop after installation.

<img src="/images/Screenmate%20-%20OneCable%20-%20Handleiding%20images/installation-images/screenmate-onecable-installation-windows-step-3.png" alt="Step 3: Install driver" className="rounded-lg border border-gray-200 dark:border-gray-700" />

### Step 4: Open display settings

Right-click on your desktop and choose Display settings.

<img src="/images/Screenmate%20-%20OneCable%20-%20Handleiding%20images/installation-images/screenmate-onecable-installation-windows-step-4.png" alt="Step 4: Display settings" className="rounded-lg border border-gray-200 dark:border-gray-700" />

### Step 5: Configure screens

Choose whether you want to extend or duplicate the screens. Are they not in the correct order? Click on Identify and drag the screens to the correct position.

<img src="/images/Screenmate%20-%20OneCable%20-%20Handleiding%20images/installation-images/screenmate-onecable-installation-windows-step-5.png" alt="Step 5: Configure screens" className="rounded-lg border border-gray-200 dark:border-gray-700" />

### Step 6: Adjust scale and resolution

Scroll down to adjust the scale, resolution, and orientation of your screen.

<img src="/images/Screenmate%20-%20OneCable%20-%20Handleiding%20images/installation-images/screenmate-onecable-installation-windows-step-6.png" alt="Step 6: Scale and resolution" className="rounded-lg border border-gray-200 dark:border-gray-700" />

<Note>
Scan the QR Code in the front of the booklet to install the driver if you cannot open the driver file.
</Note>
