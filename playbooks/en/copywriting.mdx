## Overview

This playbook describes the process for copywriting: product texts, web texts, advertisement texts, and long-form content.

## Step 1: Form Check

Check if all information is present: tone of voice, target audience, examples, purpose of the text, length, platform.

## Step 2: Intake

Schedule a call for larger projects and discuss expectations.

## Step 3: Write Concept

Copywriter creates first concept based on provided assets and brand identity.

## Step 4: Revision

Client provides feedback.
Maximum two revision rounds.

## Step 5: Final Delivery

Texts are delivered in Google Drive and shared via Slack.

## Workflow Summary

Form → Intake → First version → Revision → Delivery.
