## About Screenmate

Screenmate is an electronics company specializing in portable monitors and display solutions. We design and manufacture high-quality portable monitors that enhance productivity and provide flexible display options for professionals, students, and creative individuals.

## Our Products

### Portable Monitors
- OneCable - Ultra-portable monitor with single-cable connectivity
- Compatible with Windows, MacBooks (M1, M2, M3), and other devices
- High-resolution displays for work and entertainment

### Features
- Plug-and-play connectivity
- Lightweight and portable design
- High-quality displays
- Multiple connectivity options
- Professional-grade performance

## Our Mission

To provide innovative, high-quality portable display solutions that empower users to work and create anywhere, anytime.
